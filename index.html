ABOUT

The Winsome Experience is a detail-oriented event planning and decoration brand committed to creating unforgettable moments that exceed expectations. We specialize in weddings, birthdays, lifestyle events, and corporate functions, offering full planning, management, and styling services across Lagos and the South West states of Nigeria. From venue sourcing, vendor coordination, budget management, and stationery design to day-of coordination, we handle every detail so you can relax and enjoy your celebration.
For intimate events (up to 100 guests), we also provide bespoke decoration and venue styling that captures your unique vision. Rooted in Excellence, Transparency, and Reliability, our mission is to simplify event planning, especially for clients in the diaspora, so you can focus on what truly matters: celebrating with loved ones.
